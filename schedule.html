Lecture Schedule & Readings

The schedule below gives the planned topics for each lecture. A casual perusal of the materials before the associated lecture is recommended, followed by a more thorough reading after. Recommended readings are from the text Algorithm Design by Kleinberg and Tardos (KT) and/or the Algorithms textbook by Jeff Erickson (E).

The lecture slides and assignments will be posted here as well. The schedule may be refined and revised as the semester progresses, so please check this page regularly.
